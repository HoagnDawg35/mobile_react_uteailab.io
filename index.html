UTE AI Lab - Ho Chi Minh University of Technology and Education

The UTE AI Lab is the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory at HCMUTE, a research-driven hub focused on developing intelligent systems that can perceive, reason, and learn.

Our work spans across various areas of AI, including machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and robotics. The lab brings together faculty, students, and collaborators to tackle real-world challenges using state-of-the-art AI techniques.

Our mission is to cultivate talent, foster collaboration, and contribute impactful solutions to academia, industry, and society.
